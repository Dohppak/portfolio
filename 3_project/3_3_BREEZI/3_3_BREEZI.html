Umbrella cleaner with Wind
BRREZI
Project description

Usually we use plastic bags to cover wet umbrellas at the entrance of buildings. This causes environmental, economic problems, and messy floor. The annual plastic bag consumption for this purpose in Korea is over 100 million sheets. As Breezi uses air flow to remove rainwater, it is eco-friendly and economic. In addition, it keeps your floor clean.
